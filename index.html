Sher-E-Bangla A. K. Fazlul Huq

The Tiger of Bengal

Stalwarts of Muslim Bengal: Calcutta 1937

Here's a timeline of Sher-E-Bangla

  • 1873 - Born in Barisal, Bangladesh
  • 1897 - Completed his education with a law degree and start practice in Kolkata High Court
  • 1912 - Formed the "Central Mohammadan Educational Association” for providing students by money and education
  • 1913 - Selected as a legislative council member of Pakistan
  • 1915 - Formed an organization for farmers and laborers named "Krisho Parjasmaty" and came in favor of huge number of farmers
  • 1916 - Became president of All India Muslim league
  • 1917 - Became the joint secretary of the Indian National Congress
  • 1919 - Was made the first president of Bengal
  • 1920 - Left Congress and organized Muslim League with the help of Jinnah
  • 1935 - Became a member of the Indian Legislative Assembly and then was selected as a mayor in Kolkata Corporation
  • 1937 - Was elected as a Chief Minister of undivided Bengal
  • 1956 - Was appointed as Governor of East Pakistan
  • 1961 - Resigned from politics
  • 1962 - Passed away at the age of 89 years due to bad health
